Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 31, 2026

What Recipe Maker Does

Recipe Maker is a web application that takes recipe text you provide, uses AI to extract structured data from it, and creates a formatted Google Doc in your Google Drive.

Data We Collect

When you use Recipe Maker, we process the following:

  • Your Google account name and email (for authentication)
  • Recipe text you paste into the app

How We Use Your Data

  • Your Google account info is used solely to display your name and authenticate API requests
  • Recipe text is sent to Google Gemini AI to extract ingredients, instructions, and other recipe details
  • The extracted data is used to create a Google Doc in your Drive

Data Storage

Recipe Maker does not store your data on any server. Specifically:

  • No recipe text is saved after processing
  • No Google account information is stored server-side
  • Authentication tokens are held temporarily in your browser session and are never transmitted to or stored on our servers
  • Template and folder preferences are saved locally in your browser only

Third-Party Services

Recipe Maker uses the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication
  • Google Docs API and Google Drive API to create documents
  • Google Gemini AI to parse recipe text
  • Cloudflare for hosting and serverless functions

Each service is governed by its own privacy policy. Recipe Maker only requests the minimum permissions needed: the ability to create and edit documents and to manage files the app creates.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal data or recipe content to any third party. Data is only transmitted to Google APIs as required to provide the service.

Google API Scopes

Recipe Maker requests the following Google API scopes:

  • Google Docs — to create and format recipe documents
  • Google Drive (file-level) — to manage files created by the app and to move them to your chosen folder

The Drive scope is limited to files created by Recipe Maker. The app cannot access or modify any other files in your Drive.

Your Rights

You can revoke Recipe Maker's access to your Google account at any time by visiting your Google Account Permissions page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at the GitHub repository: github.com/lukasaurus/RecipeMaker
